Hyperbola

About

1. The dynamic content demonstrates the hyperbola and its parts.
2. The learner can observe and calculate different parts of the hyperbola.
3. This activity will help learners to understand and retain knowledge about hyperbola and its parts like center, vertex, focus, Asymptotes, intercepts, focal length, and eccentricity.

Instruction

1. Learners can see the hyperbola and its parts.
2. The buttons provided in the right top corner will help the student to view different types and forms of the hyperbola.
3. Learners can drag the values of a, b, h, and k by using sliders provided at the bottom.
4. Once the learner drags any of the values, they can see the change in the hyperbola well as the change in the values of its parts.
