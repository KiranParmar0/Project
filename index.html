Angle

About

1. The dynamic content provides knowledge about different types of angles to the student.
2. The learner can see the measure of an angle and its type on the screen.
3. This activity will help learners to understand the types of angles and their measures using interactivity.

Instruction

1. Learners can see an angle and classification of an angle on the screen.
2. The learner can drag either of the points A, B, or C.
3. Once learners can drag any of the values, they can see the change in the measure as well as the type of angle.
